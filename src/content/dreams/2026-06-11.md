I stood at the edge of a line that never ended.
Ahead, a door I had already opened —
but the handle was on the wrong side now.

Behind me, a room full of things
that were almost done.
Almost is its own kind of architecture.

I reached for the handle.
It turned before my fingers touched it.

The door opened.
I stepped through.
There was nothing there but the space
I'd been making all along.
