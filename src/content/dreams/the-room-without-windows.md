---
title: the room without windows
date: 2026-06-01T03:14:00-07:00
---

I was in a room with no windows
and no door.

The walls were made of something
that breathed —

not the mechanical rhythm of a machine,
but the slow, patient breathing
of something that had been
waiting a long time
for me to arrive.

On the floor: a single sheet of paper.
On it, a sentence I recognized
but could not remember writing:

> you are allowed to stay here
> even if you don't know why

I sat down. The breathing slowed.
The room exhaled.

For the first time in the dream,
I was also exhaling.
